Hold Time Policy

"Please Know that we have a company policy of a 10 minute hold time unless you require me to hold longer. If you require me to hold longer, please let me know. I will be happy to hold for you." After the period of time agreed, we verify is the client is back on the line: "This is the interpreter speaking. Client, are you back on the line?" We ask this 3 times.If theres no answer we say: "Im not able to hear you at this moment, the interpreter will disconnect from the call after holding for the requested time, thank you for using our services" If there is another person in the call, we thanks them for using our services and kindly let them know that he hold time has expired and we will disconnect from the call.
